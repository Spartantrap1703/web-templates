It is a period of civil wars in the galaxy. A brave alliance of underground
freedom fighters has challenged the tyranny and oppression of the awesome GALACTIC EMPIRE.

Striking from a fortress hidden among the billion stars of the galaxy, rebel
spaceships have won their first victory in a battle with the powerful Imperial Starfleet.
The EMPIRE fears that another defeat could bring a thousand more
solar systems into the rebellion, and Imperial control over the galaxy would be lost forever.

To crush the rebellion once and for all, the EMPIRE is
constructing a sinister new battle station. Powerful enough to destroy an entire
planet, its completion spells certain doom for the champions of freedom.
